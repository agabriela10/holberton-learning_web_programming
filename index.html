The beautiful Pembroke Welsh Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is a small dog breed native to Great Britain. He has served for centuries as a sheepdog, and is considered one of the oldest dogs in Britain. There is another very similar breed, the Cardigan Welsh Corgi, slightly larger and long-tailed.

He is an extremely intelligent and affectionate dog
